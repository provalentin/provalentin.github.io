# Learning Roadmap: From Zero to Interview-Ready

**Read time:** 10 min | **Actionable:** Yes

## Timeline: 12-16 Weeks

### Week 1-2: Arrays & Strings (Easy)
- Learn: arrays, strings, indexing
- Problems: 10 easy problems
- Goal: Comfortable with basics

### Week 3-4: Core Structures (Easy-Medium)
- Learn: linked lists, stacks, queues
- Problems: 15 problems
- Goal: Implement from scratch

### Week 5-6: Hashing & Trees (Easy-Medium)
- Learn: hash maps, binary trees, traversals
- Problems: 20 problems
- Goal: Tree problems feel natural

### Week 7-8: Graphs & Search (Medium)
- Learn: DFS, BFS, graphs
- Problems: 20 problems
- Goal: Understand graph patterns

### Week 9-10: DP (Medium-Hard)
- Learn: DP concepts, memoization
- Problems: 25 problems
- Goal: Recognize DP opportunities

### Week 11-12: Advanced (Hard)
- Learn: Advanced DP, segment trees, backtracking
- Problems: 20 hard problems
- Goal: Solve complex problems

### Week 13-16: Practice & Interview Prep

**Week 13: Mixed Difficulty (50 problems)**
- 10 easy (warm-up)
- 25 medium (bread and butter)
- 15 hard (stretch goals)

**Week 14: Timed Practice (10 mock interviews)**
- 45 min, one problem
- Simulate real interview
- Review solution after

**Week 15: Weak Areas**
- Identify problem types you struggle with
- Do 10-15 problems in each weak area
- Build confidence

**Week 16: Review & Confidence**
- Do 5 mock interviews
- Review data structures
- Review common patterns
- Get good sleep!

## Daily Schedule

**60 minutes per day (minimum)**

- 15 min: Review previous day's concepts
- 30 min: Solve 1-2 new problems
- 15 min: Review solution, analyze complexity

**Ideal: 90-120 minutes**
- Add: Explanation videos, related problems

## Metrics to Track

- [ ] Problems solved by difficulty (easy/medium/hard)
- [ ] Success rate (first attempt pass)
- [ ] Time to solution (vs. time limit)
- [ ] Patterns mastered (arrays, graphs, DP, etc.)

## Success Indicators

✓ Can solve medium problem in 30 min
✓ Can explain approach clearly
✓ Can identify time/space trade-offs
✓ Can suggest optimizations
✓ Handle edge cases automatically

## When Ready for Interviews

- [ ] 100+ problems solved
- [ ] Can do 1 medium problem per 30 min
- [ ] Comfortable with all core patterns
- [ ] Can explain approach clearly
- [ ] Mock interview score 7+/10

## Maintenance (After Getting Offers)

- 30 min/day: Review weak patterns
- 1-2 mock interviews/week
- Stay sharp before start date

---

**Progress > Perfection. Consistent practice beats cramming.**
